📝 Written Description

Maplewood is a lively and welcoming neighborhood located in the heart of the city. It has everything a resident could need: a supermarket and a pharmacy on Maple Avenue, a beautiful park at the center, and a school just a block north. The neighborhood is arranged around a central square with a fountain, making it easy to find your way around. I particularly like the café on the corner of Oak Street — it is cozy and five minutes on foot from home. Overall, Maplewood feels safe, walkable, and full of life at every corner.
