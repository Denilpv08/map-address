📝 Written Description

Maplewood is a lively and welcoming neighborhood located in the heart of the city. It has everything a resident could need: a supermarket and a pharmacy on Maple Avenue, a beautiful park at the center, and a school just a block north. The neighborhood is arranged around a central square with a fountain, making it easy to find your way around. I particularly like the café on the corner of Oak Street — it is cozy and five minutes on foot from home. Overall, Maplewood feels safe, walkable, and full of life at every corner.

🎙️ Audio Script – Route to the Park (max 2 min) Read this script clearly and record it on your phone or computer: "Hello! Welcome to Maplewood. I will explain how to get to the park from my home. Start at Home, on Maple Avenue. Go straight on Maple Avenue heading north for one block. At the traffic light, turn right onto Oak Street. Walk two blocks east and cross the street at the pedestrian crossing. The park is right in front of you, next to the fountain in the central square. It takes about five minutes on foot. Enjoy the park!"
